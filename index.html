Who we are

ACS is the largest and the most active community help group for the students of AIUB. ​​We are a group of people consisting of Ex-students and students of AIUB. Omar Faruk & Rakib H Chowdhury from 14-2 & 15-1 batch of AIUB felt the necessity of proper guideline for a student to pursue his/her dreams. Therefore, they came up with an innovative idea to gather people who will work for the students selflessly. Thus, we ended up forming ACS!

What we do

We help AIUB students in the best possible way within the shortest possible time. Since it’s inception back in 2015, ACS has been working selflessly for a decade with a generous team monitoring the Facebook group of 40K+ people 24/7 and helping thousands of people every month.
